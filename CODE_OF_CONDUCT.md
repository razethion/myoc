# Code of Conduct

MyOC project spaces should stay practical, respectful, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being hostile.
- Keep technical discussions focused on evidence, tradeoffs, and project goals.
- Respect maintainers' time and decisions.
- Credit other people's work accurately.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Sexualized language, sexual imagery, or unwanted sexual attention.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Spam, bad-faith participation, or repeated off-topic demands.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report conduct concerns to the repository owner privately with relevant links, screenshots, dates, and a concise
description.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, block users, or take other reasonable action when
behavior violates this code.

Security vulnerabilities should be reported through [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md), not through conduct reports or public
issues.
