Product basics

Quick answers

MyOC has one job: deliver original-quality media to users who want it, with a simple interface and no gimmicks.

Q

Why use MyOC?

MyOC is a gallery for character media and assets. It allows you to do so simply, effectively, and without unnessecary extra gimmicks.

Q

Does it preserve image quality?

Yes. The focus is original-resolution uploads and clear previews, so high-detail artwork can be inspected instead of flattened into low-quality thumbnails. We only do minor, detail and colorspace-preserving edits to ensure the integrity of the original artwork.

Q

What's wrong with <other gallery site>?

MyOC does one thing: display media. No literature, no custom CSS, no gimmicks. Other sites don't retain quality, vomit watermarks all over your images, or haven't been updated in years. MyOC makes it easy to share media with others while maintaining the integrity of the original artwork.

Q

Do you allow NSFW content?

Yes, as long as it falls within our acceptable use policy. MyOC will never ban users for NSFW content. We believe in freedom of expression, but we also prioritize the safety and well-being of our community.
